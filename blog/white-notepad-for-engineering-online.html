Think of it as scratch paper that lives in your browser. You can access it from any device, any time.

Why Engineers Use Online Notepads

Common Use Cases

Quick Calculations

Write out formulas, list variables, or outline an algorithm before opening your IDE. It's faster than starting a Jupyter notebook for a quick thought.

Code Snippets

Keep terminal commands, SQL queries, or config strings in an always-open tab. Saves you from searching through old Slack messages every time.

Meeting Notes

Capture action items during design reviews. No formatting to fight with. After the meeting, click Share and drop the link in your team chat.

Documentation Drafts

Outline specs or reports in plain text first. Focus on content, not layout. Move it to your formal tool when you're ready to polish.
